Fundraising for the East Coast Greenway Alliance

Supporting a trail from Maine to Florida

I serve as Board Chair of the East Coast Greenway Alliance, a non-profit dedicated to building an accessible, off-road trail connecting Maine to Florida. I biked this trail in 2021, it was a life-changing experience and this is my way of giving back :)

To fundraise, I've made a series of hand-crafted rewards. Donate through my fundraising link and send me proof, and I'll mail you your item of choice!

Currently mailing to US addresses only.
