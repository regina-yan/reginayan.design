SuperDial —
AI Healthcare Phone Caller

Designing and building an automated AI phone caller that handles healthcare administrative calls — reducing hours of manual work for providers into seconds.

Overview

SuperDial is an AI healthcare startup building tools to automate the tedious, time-consuming phone calls that healthcare providers have to make every day — from insurance verifications to prior authorizations. These calls can take 45+ minutes each and pull clinical staff away from patients.

I joined as a designer and engineer, responsible for the full product experience as well as the marketing site in Webflow.

The Problem

Healthcare administrative work is notoriously manual. Staff spend enormous amounts of time on hold, navigating phone trees, and re-entering data — all for routine tasks that follow predictable patterns. SuperDial's AI system could handle these calls automatically, but the product needed a clear, trustworthy interface to configure, monitor, and review those calls.

Design & Build

My work spanned both the core product interface and the public-facing Webflow marketing site. On the product side, I designed the call configuration flow, monitoring dashboard, and results review experience — balancing clarity with the complexity of healthcare workflows. On the engineering side, I contributed to both front-end and back-end implementation, allowing me to ship my own designs directly.

Outcome

SuperDial launched and began onboarding healthcare providers, automating calls that previously consumed hours of staff time per day. The tight design-to-engineering loop allowed the team to move quickly without sacrificing quality.
