Supporting a trail from Maine to Florida

In 2021, I embarked on a life-changing solo biking trip from Maine to Florida on the East Coast Greenway. This ride taught me the joys of muscle-powered movement and that we're all stronger than we think. I've since joined the East Coast Greenway Alliance as board chair to help continue their mission of building a nationally connected, locally rooted trail.

Now, 5 years after my ride, I happened to get laid off right as I'm feeling the itch to do another long-distance bike trip. Being unemployed sucks, but when life hands you lemons, go have a Euro-girl summer!

I'll be riding for 30 days in France with a few overlaps on the official Tour de France route (specifically the mountain stages!) Unlike last time, I don't have anything to prove, so I'm designing each day around what I want to do: ride my bike, have fun, make art, and give back to the East Coast Greenway Alliance so that more people have access to safe outdoors spaces.

Currently mailing to US addresses only. Everything will ship in mid-August when I return from France.
