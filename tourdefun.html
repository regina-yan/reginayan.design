Ya girl's going global

In 2021, I embarked on a solo biking trip from Maine to Florida on the East Coast Greenway. I'm not exaggerating when I say it was life-changing: pushing myself extremely out of my comfort zone made me the strong, confident, biking-obsessed woman I am today. I've since joined the East Coast Greenway Alliance as board chair to help continue their mission of building a nationally connected, locally rooted trail.

Now, 5 years after my ride, I happen to get laid off right as I'm feeling the itch to do another long-distance bike trip. Being unemployed sucks, but when life hands you lemons, go have a Euro-girl summer!

I'll be riding for 30 days around France with a few overlaps on the official Tour de France route (specifically the mountain stages)! But let's not forget, this is a Tour de Fun, so I'm designing each day around what I want to do: ride my bike, create art, and give back to the East Coast Greenway Alliance so that more people have access to safe outdoors spaces.

Currently mailing to US addresses only. Everything will ship in mid-August when I return from France.
